Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence 1 is a comprehensive, interactive learning platform designed to introduce high school students to the fascinating world of AI, machine learning, and computational thinking.

Instructor

Mr. Williams, MSCS (Master of Science in Computer Science)
Your guide through the world of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

What You'll Learn

This course covers 6 major topics:

  • AI Ethics - Responsible AI development and usage
  • Data Science - Data visualization and analysis
  • Machine Learning - Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
  • Deep Learning - Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, Transformers
  • Search Algorithms - Pathfinding and optimization
  • Game AI - Strategic decision making and game trees
