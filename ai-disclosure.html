About This Platform

This interactive learning platform for Mr. Williams' AI 1 class was created with significant assistance from artificial intelligence, specifically Claude (Anthropic's AI assistant). We believe in transparency about the tools and methods used in educational content creation.

Full Disclosure: All source code, interactive visualizations, lessons, and games on this platform were generated with AI assistance using Claude by Anthropic.

How AI Was Used

AI assistance was utilized throughout the development process to create high-quality, educational content for high school students. Here's how:

  • Code Generation: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code for all interactive visualizers, games, and lessons were written with AI assistance
  • Educational Content: Explanations, descriptions, and instructional text were crafted to be age-appropriate and pedagogically sound
  • Algorithm Implementation: Complex AI algorithms (machine learning, search, optimization, game AI, and constraint satisfaction) were implemented as interactive demonstrations
  • User Interface Design: Consistent, accessible, and engaging UI/UX design across all 40+ tools and visualizers
  • Testing & Debugging: AI helped identify and fix issues to ensure all tools function correctly

Human Oversight

While AI generated the code and content, human oversight was maintained throughout:

  • Pedagogical Direction: All learning objectives, tool requirements, and educational goals were defined by human educators
  • Review & Refinement: Generated content was reviewed and refined based on educational standards and student needs
  • Quality Control: All tools were tested to ensure they accurately demonstrate AI concepts and algorithms
  • Curriculum Integration: Tools align with high school AI curriculum and learning standards

Educational Value

The use of AI in creating this platform demonstrates several important lessons for students:

  • AI as a Tool: AI can be a powerful tool for productivity and creation when used thoughtfully and ethically
  • Human-AI Collaboration: The best results come from combining human expertise, creativity, and judgment with AI capabilities
  • Transparency: Being open about AI use builds trust and models ethical practices in technology
  • Understanding AI: Students using these tools learn AI concepts while also seeing practical applications of AI in education

Teaching Philosophy: We believe students should understand that AI is a tool that augments human capability rather than replaces it. This platform itself is an example of how AI can enhance education while maintaining human direction and values.
