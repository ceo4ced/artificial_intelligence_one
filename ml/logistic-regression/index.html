üìö What is Logistic Regression?

Logistic regression is a classification algorithm that predicts which category something belongs to. Unlike linear regression, it outputs probabilities between 0 and 1, making it perfect for yes/no decisions.

Real-world applications:

  • Email spam detection (spam vs. not spam)
  • Medical diagnosis (disease vs. healthy)
  • Student pass/fail prediction based on study time
  • Credit approval (approve vs. deny)
